# VoxTics Product Overview

VoxTics is a comprehensive cinema booking system built with ASP.NET Core MVC. The application provides a complete movie theater management solution with both customer-facing and administrative interfaces.

## Core Features

- **Movie Management**: Browse movies, view details, showtimes, and ratings
- **Cinema & Hall Management**: Multiple cinema locations with configurable halls and seating
- **Booking System**: Complete ticket booking workflow with seat selection and payment processing
- **User Management**: Customer accounts, authentication, and profile management
- **Admin Panel**: Full administrative interface for managing movies, cinemas, bookings, and users
- **Payment Integration**: Stripe payment processing for ticket purchases
- **Notification System**: Email notifications and in-app messaging
- **Watchlist**: Users can save movies to their personal watchlist

## Key Business Entities

- **Movies**: Core content with categories, actors, images, and showtimes
- **Cinemas**: Physical locations with multiple halls
- **Showtimes**: Scheduled movie screenings with pricing
- **Bookings**: Customer reservations with seat assignments
- **Users**: Customer accounts with authentication and profiles
- **Cart System**: Shopping cart functionality for ticket purchases

## Target Users

- **Customers**: Movie-goers booking tickets online
- **Cinema Staff**: Managing day-to-day operations
- **Administrators**: Full system management and analytics
